# 📰 TrueLens AI – AI-Powered News Verification & Transparency Platform

## Overview

TrueLens AI is a full-stack web application designed to combat misinformation by helping users verify the authenticity of news content. Users can browse categorized news, submit news articles, URLs, text, or image-based news for verification, and receive AI-assisted insights on the credibility of the information.

The platform compares submitted content with trusted news sources and provides a credibility assessment along with supporting information, promoting transparency and informed decision-making.

---

## Features

* Browse news by categories:

  * 🌍 International
  * 🇮🇳 National
  * 🏛 Politics
  * ⚽ Sports
  * 💻 Technology

* Verify:

  * News Articles
  * URLs
  * Text Content

* AI-assisted credibility analysis

* Confidence score for verification
